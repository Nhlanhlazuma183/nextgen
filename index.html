Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do I need for online tutoring?
For online sessions, you'll need a computer or tablet with internet access and a microphone. A digital stylus can be helpful for working through problems, but it's not required. All digital materials will be provided.
How are group classes structured?
Group classes have 3-5 students of similar ability levels. Sessions are 1-2 hours long, typically once or twice a week. We cover the curriculum systematically while allowing time for individual questions and collaborative problem-solving.
Do you help with exam preparation?
Yes! Exam preparation is a key part of my tutoring. We work on past papers, exam techniques, time management, and identifying areas that need extra focus. I provide customized revision schedules for major exams.
What is your cancellation policy?
I require at least 24 hours notice for session cancellations. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours notice may be charged at 50% of the session fee. Monthly group classes follow a fixed schedule with make-up sessions available for missed classes.
