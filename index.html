About Me

I am a passionate and dedicated tutor with a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering. I transform the way students perceive and excel in Mathematics and Physical Sciences through personalized, engaging, and practical tutoring sessions.

With 6+ years of experience guiding students from Grade 8 to 12, I have developed a method that simplifies complex concepts and builds unshakable foundational confidence. My engineering background allows me to bridge textbook theory with real-world application, showing students the "why" behind the formulas and making learning both relevant and exciting.

My Mission

To end the stigma that Mathematics and Physics are inherently difficult by providing a clear, patient, and supportive learning environment. I am committed to unlocking each student's potential through tailored explanations, practical context, and confidence-building success.

My Vision

To create a generation of students who approach quantitative subjects not with anxiety, but with curiosity and confidence, empowered with the logical skills and problem-solving abilities that will serve them in any academic or career path they choose.
